ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm a Software Engineering student at McMaster University, expected to graduate in 2022. Welcome to my website! You might be wondering why it's so artsy for someone who's in software, well all the artwork is made by my artist sister Khadija Aziz. I just think it's pretty, that's all!

I'm interested in lots of different aspects of computer and software engineering, namely backend software development, cloud computing, distributed computing, embedded systems, digital circuits, operating systems and computer architecture! I know that's a lot of topics, but I'm hoping to get experience working with all of these to figure out what I want to focus in on for the rest of my career!

Although I'm going into my third year, I've actually been in university for 4 years. I was originally in Computer Engineering, but later I switched into Software Engineering. So I have lots of technical projects under my belt and I've learned lots of theory too! I've had two internships which I'll talk more about in the Internship section. This summer of 2020, I'm working at Amazon (Vancouver) remotely from my house in Ajax, Ontario. During my free time I like to explore lots of cool tech - I recently bought a DE10-Nano board which has an FPGA and an ARM processor that I'll hopefully do some fun projects with! I've also been working on some small personal software projects, you can check them out on GitHub.
